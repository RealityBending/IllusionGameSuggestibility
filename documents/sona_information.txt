**2 credits** | 30min online study | Can you resist visual illusions?

This study aims at validating a reaction time task based on visual illusions

In this study, you will be required to complete various questionnaires about yourself, as well as play the Illusion Game. In this task, the role is to make decisions by pressing a key as fast as possible without being distracted by illusions.
